## Why Mantou?

- **Diagrams as data.** Every <Code>&lt;Flow&gt;</Code> parses into a graph AST, a
  relationship index, runtime state, and reactive style rules.
- **Reactive styling.** Appearance depends on <Code>node.on</Code>,{" "}
  <Code>node.parent.selected</Code>, <Code>node.children.any(on)</Code>, and more.
- **Safe by construction.** A tiny expression language — no <Code>eval</Code>, no
  globals, no filesystem beyond a scoped <Code>package.json</Code> read.
- **Interactive.** Selection, hover, focus, dependency expansion, and live relayout,
  built in.

<Callout type="note" title="Not Mermaid">
  Mantou uses React Flow for rendering instead of producing a flat image, so
  diagrams stay interactive and reactive inside your documentation.
</Callout>
